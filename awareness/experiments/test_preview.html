Edition Focus
This edition focuses on three active threats targeting enterprise environments — phishing campaigns impersonating senior leaders, a new ransomware variant in the healthcare sector, and a surge in password-spraying attacks. Stay alert and report anything unusual.
 
PRIORITY
01
Phishing HIGH
Phishing Wave Hits Finance Teams
Attackers are sending fake CFO emails to trick finance staff into authorising fraudulent wire transfers worth millions.
↗ Read more — BleepingComputer
Action Items
›Verify all wire requests via phone callback
›Check sender domain carefully before replying
 
PRIORITY
02
Ransomware CRITICAL
Ransomware Gang Targets Healthcare
A new ransomware variant is encrypting hospital records and demanding payment before releasing access to patient data.
↗ Read more — Krebs on Security
Action Items
›Back up critical data offline regularly
›Disconnect infected systems immediately and notify IT
 
PRIORITY
03
Password & MFA ELEVATED
Password Spraying Attacks Surge Across Sector
Adversaries are attempting to gain access by trying common passwords across thousands of accounts simultaneously.
↗ Read more — SecurityWeek
Action Items
›Enable MFA on all accounts without exception
›Use a password manager to ensure unique passwords
