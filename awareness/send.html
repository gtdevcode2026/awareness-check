Email delivery (Microsoft Graph or SMTP relay) is configured in Configuration. Update recipients or relay details there, then return here to send.
Advisories (Home → Advisory) — two no-relay options. Build Advisory Sender (.py): nessus-style script; run python send_advisories.py, enter sender + recipients, sends one email per advisory. Advisory → EML (Outlook): downloads one combined .eml — double-click to open it in Outlook as a draft (images inline), confirm recipients, then Send (no app password — Outlook sends as you).
Prefer to send from Outlook? Download a send-ready file, double-click to open it (images inline), add recipients, then Send — no relay needed.
